Welcome to Inner Bloom Mental Wellness

I'm Amanda Spirek, and at Inner Bloom Mental Wellness, I believe healing is a quiet blooming. It is a return to wholeness through compassion, curiosity, and courage. I walk beside those navigating life’s seasons, offering a space where stories unfold and new growth can begin.

My mission is to help you reclaim your story, soften self-doubt, and reconnect with the strength that lives within. I guide each person toward healing, wholeness, and the quiet bloom of becoming.

Amanda Spirek, Licensed Mental Health Counselor, MC, LPC, NCC

Licensed Professional Counselor in Arizona & Indiana
EMDR Level 1 & 2 Trained

My approach to therapy is a gentle, supportive, and collaborative space where you feel safe and understood. Using tools like EMDR, mindfulness, and body awareness, we’ll explore what’s beneath the surface. Whether you’re healing old wounds, facing life changes, or wanting to feel more like yourself, we’ll move at the pace of trust.
