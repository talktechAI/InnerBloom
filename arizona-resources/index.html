Arizona Mental Health Resources

As a Licensed Professional Counselor in Arizona, I understand the unique mental health landscape and resources available in our state. Here are valuable resources for Arizona residents seeking mental health support.

Crisis Support

Arizona Crisis Line: 1-844-534-4673

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 988

Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741

24/7 crisis support for individuals experiencing mental health emergencies.

Arizona Department of Health Services

The state's behavioral health authority provides resources, programs, and support for mental health and substance abuse services throughout Arizona.

Website: azdhs.gov/behavioral-health-services

NAMI Arizona

National Alliance on Mental Illness Arizona chapter provides education, support groups, and advocacy for individuals and families affected by mental illness.

Website: namiaz.org

Arizona Trauma Institute

Specialized training and resources for trauma-informed care and EMDR therapy.

Website: aztrauma.org

211 Arizona

Dial 2-1-1 or visit their website for comprehensive information about health and human services, including mental health resources, throughout Arizona.

Website: 211arizona.org

Insurance and Financial Resources

Understanding your insurance coverage and finding affordable mental health care is crucial. Here are Arizona-specific resources:

  • AHCCCS (Arizona Health Care Cost Containment System): Arizona's Medicaid program providing mental health coverage
  • Healthcare.gov: Find and compare health insurance plans with mental health coverage
