Indiana Mental Health Resources

As a Licensed Professional Counselor serving Indiana residents via telehealth, I'm committed to connecting you with local resources and support systems in the Hoosier State.

Crisis Support

Indiana Crisis Line: 1-800-985-5990

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 988

Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741

24/7 crisis support for individuals experiencing mental health emergencies.

Indiana Family and Social Services Administration

The state's division of mental health and addiction provides comprehensive behavioral health services and resources.

Website: in.gov/fssa/dmha/

Indiana Professional Licensing Agency

Verify licensed mental health professionals and find qualified therapists in Indiana.

Website: in.gov/pla/professions/mental-health-counselor/

NAMI Indiana

National Alliance on Mental Illness Indiana chapter provides education, support groups, and advocacy for individuals and families affected by mental illness.

Website: namiindiana.org

Mental Health America of Indiana

Advocacy, education, and direct services for mental health awareness and support.

Website: mhai.net

211 Indiana

Dial 2-1-1 or visit their website for comprehensive information about health and human services, including mental health resources, throughout Indiana.

Website: 211.org

Regional Support Centers

Indiana has several regional mental health centers providing local support:

  • Northeast Indiana: Northeast Indiana Regional Coordinating Council
  • Central Indiana: Midtown Community Mental Health
  • Northwest Indiana: Region Mental Health Center
  • Southern Indiana: Southern Hills Counseling Center

Insurance and Financial Resources

Understanding your insurance coverage and finding affordable mental health care:

  • Hoosier Healthwise: Indiana's Medicaid program providing mental health coverage
  • Healthcare.gov: Find and compare health insurance plans with mental health coverage
