Services

I offer comprehensive mental health services designed to meet you where you are in your healing journey.

Individual Therapy

Personalized therapy sessions addressing anxiety, depression, life transitions, relationship issues, and personal growth. I use an integrative approach tailored to your specific needs and preferences.

Therapeutic Approaches:

  • CBT - Cognitive Behavioral Therapy
  • EMDR - Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing
  • Mindfulness - Present-moment awareness techniques
  • Solution-focused - Goal-oriented therapy

EMDR Therapy

Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR) is an evidence-based treatment for trauma and PTSD. As a Level 1 and 2 trained practitioner, I specialize in helping clients process traumatic experiences.

Telehealth Sessions

Secure, convenient online therapy sessions that allow you to receive support from the comfort of your own space. Available for residents of Arizona and Indiana.

Trauma-Informed Care

All of my services are provided through a trauma-informed lens, recognizing the impact of trauma and prioritizing safety, trustworthiness, and collaboration in our work together.

Areas of Specialty

I have specialized training and experience in working with:

Trauma/PTSD
Evidence-based treatment for trauma and post-traumatic stress using EMDR and trauma-informed approaches
Infertility
Compassionate support for the emotional challenges of fertility struggles, loss, and family-building journeys
Post-partum
Specialized care for postpartum depression, anxiety, and adjustment challenges during the transition to motherhood
Life Transitions
Guidance through major life changes including career shifts, relationship changes, loss, and personal growth
Anxiety
Treatment for generalized anxiety, panic disorders, social anxiety, and phobias using evidence-based techniques
LGBTQIA+
Affirming therapy for gender identity, sexual orientation, coming out, and LGBTQIA+ relationship issues

Clinical Supervision

Are you a Licensed Associate Counselor in Arizona looking for a supervisor that is supportive, growth-oriented, and committed to your professional development? I offer clinical supervision that focuses on:

  • Supportive guidance through your licensure journey
  • Growth-oriented feedback and mentorship
  • Professional development and skill building
  • Ethical decision-making and best practices
